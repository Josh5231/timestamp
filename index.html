Time Stamp Microservice

By: Josh Sanders

This microservice provides a natural and unix time object when one is passed to it.

Examples:

https://obscure-thicket-76275.herokuapp.com/March 10, 2016
https://obscure-thicket-76275.herokuapp.com/1457568000000

Will both produce: {"unix":1457568000000,"natural":"March 10, 2016"}



This was made for FreeCodeCamp.com's Timestamp Microservice project.
